How to Install RAM

Follow these steps to install RAM in your computer:

1. Turn off your computer and unplug it from the power source.

2. Open the computer's case by removing the screws or clips securing the side panel.

3. Locate the RAM slots on the motherboard. These are usually near the CPU.

4. Release the clips on either side of the RAM slot to make room for the new RAM stick.

5. Align the RAM stick with the slot, ensuring it’s correctly oriented.

6. Gently press the RAM stick into the slot until the clips snap into place.

7. Close the case, plug the computer back in, and power it on.

8. Check the system’s BIOS or operating system to confirm the RAM has been installed properly.

How to Perform a Windows Update

Follow these steps to perform a Windows update:

1. Click on the "Start" button (Windows icon) on your taskbar.

2. Go to "Settings" by clicking the gear icon.

3. In the "Settings" window, select "Update & Security".

4. Click on "Windows Update" in the left-hand menu.

5. If updates are available, you will see an option to "Download and Install".

6. Click the "Download" button to begin the update process.

7. Once downloaded, click "Restart Now" to complete the installation of updates.

8. Wait for your computer to restart and finish the update installation. It may take a few minutes.

9. Once complete, your computer will be up to date with the latest Windows updates.
